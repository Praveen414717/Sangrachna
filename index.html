About Us

Sangrachna, meaning "to create," is the editorial club at the Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology, Greater Noida. It provides students a platform to express themselves through writing, hosting discussions, open mics, and various extracurricular events. The club encourages students to overcome hesitation and stage fright while keeping them informed about global issues, motivating them to engage in inter-college competitions and take active roles beyond the classroom.

Kitabghar

Kitabghar is an initiative by the Sangrachna Club, creating a haven for bibliophiles within our institution. This cozy library is more than just a collection; it's a community-driven resource, offering a range of novels and literary treasures to rent. Kitabghar was built with the goal of fostering a love for reading, where stories await every curious reader, and everyone has access to literature that sparks imagination and insight.
